Privacy Policy  •  Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Tiny Steps ("we", "our", or "us") is a children's habit-building and educational app designed for families. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and parents who use our application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Parent or guardian email address (used to save and restore app data)
  • Child's first name (used only for personalised greetings within the app)
  • Parent's name (used within the app for the family section)
  • A 4-digit parent PIN (stored locally and in the cloud, encrypted)

Information generated by app use:

  • Task completion history
  • Quiz scores and progress
  • Puzzle completion records
  • Daily mood check-ins
  • Coin balance and reward requests
  • Streak tracking data

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect real names of children beyond a first name
  • We do NOT collect photos, videos, or audio
  • We do NOT collect device location
  • We do NOT collect contacts or phone numbers
  • We do NOT use advertising networks or sell data to third parties
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties for marketing

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your app progress across devices
  • To enable the parent approval workflow within your family
  • To display personalised greetings and progress tracking
  • To allow data recovery after reinstalling the app
  • To enable the mood history feature for parents to view

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the app.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Tiny Steps is designed for use by children under parental supervision. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We only collect a child's first name, provided by a parent or guardian
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent
  • Parents can request deletion of all data at any time by contacting us
  • No child data is shared with third parties
  • The app does not allow children to communicate with strangers

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — using Android SharedPreferences for fast local access
  • In the cloud — using Google Firebase Firestore, secured by Firebase security rules. Data is accessible only via your registered email address.

We use industry-standard security practices. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the app. If you would like your data deleted, please contact us at the email below and we will remove all associated records within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — for cloud data storage and sync. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — for subscription payments. Google's privacy policy applies to all payment processing.

We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs.

8. Your Rights

As a parent or guardian you have the right to:

  • Access all data stored about your family
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request complete deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the app

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
